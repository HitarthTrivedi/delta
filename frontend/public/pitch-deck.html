03 / 12 The Solution

One brain. Three agents.
A loop that never stops.

Agent 1

Intake & Resume Intelligence

A warm, adaptive AI conversation — not a form. It reads your resume, understands your goals, constraints, weekly hours, exam pressure, and learning style. Output is structured career intelligence, not a transcript. Works for a 12th grader, a dropout, a career-switcher, and a master's candidate equally.

Agent 2

Weekly Roadmap Strategist

Builds only the next week. Chooses between a course, a project, a paper, a language class, a revision block, or a full exam pause — based on pace, deadlines, what you finished, and what the market demands right now. Talk to it mid-week and it rewrites the plan immediately. No generic checklist. No forgetting.

Agent 3

Progress & Opportunity Controller

Studies all completed work, keeps the dashboard current, surfaces internships and live signals, improves resume positioning, and turns completed proof into a clear visual career record. It grades your portfolio honestly — what's strong, what's weak, what to fix before you apply.
